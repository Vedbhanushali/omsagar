It's Called TFO

At the yarn stage, TFO renders improved evenness, strength and elongation the material. It also enhances the lustre and abrasion resistance of the yarn. At the fabric stage, however TFO aids in attaining higher stability, better handle, visual characteristics and improved fabric roll.
